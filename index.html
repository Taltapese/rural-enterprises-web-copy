FARMING JOBS AUSTRALIA

If you are looking for permanent or seasonal farm work in Australia, then please check our farm jobs page. We specialise in assisting Australian candidates, overseas candidates and working holiday visa holders find rural employment on farms in seasonal harvest and seeding jobs and also in permanent positions. Candidates with agricultural experience in tractor, header/combine or truck driving, are encouraged to register for farm work opportunities with us.

Further information on requirements and what to expect from rural work in Australia can be found on our Working in Australia page. We can also refer you to a Migration Consultant should you have questions regarding working visas.

RURAL RECRUITMENT & FARM WORKERS

If you are a Farm Manager or owner looking to find experienced farm workers, then please visit our rural employmentpage to register your information and job requirements. One of our team will be in touch shortly to confirm your recruitment needs.

Rural Enterprises Australia specialise in sourcing seasonal farm workers as well as permanent farm employees to assist you in meeting your staffing requirements. Visit our Featured Candidates page to see just a handful of those workers looking for their next opportunity.
